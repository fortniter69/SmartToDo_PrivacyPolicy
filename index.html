SmartToDo+ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to SmartToDo+! We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains how SmartToDo+ collects, uses, and discloses information, particularly information obtained through the integration with Google Calendar.

Information We Collect and Google API Usage

When you choose to integrate SmartToDo+ with your Google Account, we may request access to the following information for the sole purpose of providing and improving our habit tracking and synchronization features:

  • Google User ID and Email: Used to authenticate your account and identify you for data storage.
  • Google Calendar Read/Write Access: We access your Calendar Metadata (e.g., calendar names) and Event Data (e.g., event titles, descriptions, start/end times) to automatically block out time for your habits or mark habits as completed based on your scheduled events.

Important Notice Regarding Google User Data: SmartToDo+'s use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

How We Use Your Information

Your information, including Google User Data, is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core habit synchronization service (e.g., scheduling habits into your Google Calendar).
  • To authenticate you and manage your preferences within SmartToDo+.
  • For internal operations, such as troubleshooting and data analysis to improve app features.

Sharing and Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your Personal Information to others. Specifically, SmartToDo+ will **never** transfer Google user data to third parties, sell it, or use it for serving advertisements.

We may disclose your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., in response to a subpoena or government request).
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users or the public.

Data Security and Retention

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the SmartToDo+ services and for legitimate and essential business purposes, such as maintaining the app's performance.

Your Rights and Revoking Access

You have the right to withdraw your consent and revoke SmartToDo+'s access to your Google Calendar data at any time. You can do this either directly through the SmartToDo+ app settings, or by visiting your Google Account security settings and managing third-party app access there. Revoking access will prevent SmartToDo+ from performing future synchronization actions.
