Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartToDo+, a habit tracking and productivity application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our services, including when you connect your Google Calendar.

By using SmartToDo+, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Information you provide when creating an account, such as your email address and username.
  • Habit and Task Data: Information related to the habits you track, including frequency, completion status, titles, and scheduled times.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you access and use the service, such as device type, browser information, and activity timestamps.
  • Google User Data (Calendar): As detailed below in Section 3.

3. Google API Services User Data Policy

SmartToDo+ uses Google Calendar API Services to provide features that enhance your habit tracking, such as displaying your existing schedule to avoid conflicts.

3.1. Data Accessed and Scope

We access your Google Calendar data using the scopes required to read and potentially write your calendar events and metadata. We only access the data necessary to provide the calendar integration feature.

3.2. How Data is Used

  • Conflict Checking: To display your existing calendar commitments alongside your planned habits, helping you schedule realistically.
  • Habit Creation: To allow you to easily add new habits as events to your calendar (if you choose to enable the write feature).
  • Display Only: The content of your calendar events is processed internally to check for conflicts but is not stored on our servers, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose outside of providing the SmartToDo+ application features.

3.3. Restricted Use Disclosure

SmartToDo+'s use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • WE DO NOT transfer, sell, or distribute your Google user data to third parties.
  • WE DO NOT use your Google user data for serving advertisements.
  • WE DO NOT use your Google user data for any purpose other than providing and improving the core functionalities of SmartToDo+.

3.4. Data Retention and Deletion

Your Google Calendar data is accessed in real-time and is not permanently stored by SmartToDo+. Revoking access to your Google Calendar through your Google Account settings will immediately stop all access by our application.

4. How We Use the Information

We use the collected data for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the SmartToDo+ service.
  • To notify you about changes to our service.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To monitor the usage of the service for performance and stability.
  • To enforce our terms, conditions, and policies.

5. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard measures to protect your personal data, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
